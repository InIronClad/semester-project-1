Why Christmas is on the 25th History.com This video discusses why Christmas is on the 25th. Looking at the information available, Jesus' birth was most likely in June. The reason it is on the 25th is that in Roman times the festival of Saturnalia occurred around the same time. By setting Christmas time about the same time period as well, the early Church could bring more followers in.
