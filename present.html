Christmas in the Present Day

Christmastide

Christmas today depends on who the person is. If someone is part of Catholicism, then Christmas would be celebrated very differently from the secular traditions. They observe Advent in the weeks leading up to Christmas, and go to a special mass on Christmas day. From Christmas day until the sunday following Jan. 6th, though it can fall on the following Monday if Sunday is the Epiphany. The Epiphany is, "the manifestation of Christ to the Gentiles by the Magi (Matthew 2:1-12)."

After Christmas Day

For Catholicism, Christmastime does not end after Christmas day. The, "christmas season," lasts until the Epipany or until Jan. 6th.
